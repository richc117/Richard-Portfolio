Welcome! To my increasingly awesome portfolio

My Name is Richard Caballero. I am an interaction designer and current student at the UC San Diego. I have been working at the Interactive Cognition Lab for the last year under the guidance of Professor David Kirsh. This where I have learned first hand that design is not about forcing my own views on the world, I am not an Artist. The most important thing I have learned through my education and work is how to solve problems. I thrive within a constrained environment, I hate compromises, and I employ artistic methods to visualize thinking and process. I am a designer and that means I solve problems and I add aesthetic twist while I'm at it!
